Frequently Asked Questions

What is JS Chain Lending collateralization?
JS Chain Lending collateralization enables users to use assets from different blockchains as collateral for loans.
Is the platform secure?
Yes, our platform uses decentralized and verified smart contracts for maximum security.
What stable assets can I borrow?
You can borrow a variety of stablecoins, including USDT, USDC, and more.
How can I add collateral to the platform?
You can add collateral by connecting your wallet and selecting the assets you want to use as collateral from supported blockchains.
How are the interest rates calculated?
Interest rates are calculated based on the type of collateral and market conditions. We strive to offer competitive and fair rates.
Can I withdraw my collateral at any time?
Yes, you can withdraw your collateral at any time, provided you have repaid the loan in full.
